# 🤖 Bot Lab — build your own AI chatbot!

Welcome to Tech Camp! Today you're going to build a chatbot with its own personality.

## How to start
1. Click **Run** (top of the screen). Your bot appears in the preview window.
2. Open **`bot-config.js`** — this is your bot's "DNA" and the only file you edit.
3. Change something (your bot's name, color, or personality), then hit **Run** again
   (or refresh the preview) to see it change.

## The only file you edit: `bot-config.js`
- **BOT_NAME / ACCENT_COLOR** — what your bot is called and its color.
- **SYSTEM_PROMPT** — your bot's secret personality instructions (this is the fun part).
- **USE_KNOWLEDGE / KNOWLEDGE_BASE** — give your bot facts it can look up (Block 3).
- **OFFLINE_MODE** — set to `true` if the wifi dies, so you can keep working.

Everything else (`index.html`, `server.js`) already works — leave it alone.

Have fun, and try to give your bot the weirdest personality you can think of!
